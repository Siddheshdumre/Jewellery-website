About Us

About Ambadas Faye Jewellers

Established in 1982, Ambadas Faye Jewellers is a family-owned business that has flourished through generations. Our journey began with a vision to create jewellery that is an epitome of craftsmanship, elegance, and love. Over the years, we have honed our skills and garnered a loyal clientele who trust us for our authenticity and impeccable designs. What sets us apart is our commitment to excellence and personalized service. Our team of skilled artisans and designers pour their heart and soul into every creation, ensuring that each piece tells a unique story. At Ambadas Faye Jewellers, we believe in celebrating love, life, and the moments that matter. Whether you're commemorating a milestone or expressing your affection, our jewellery is crafted to perfection, making it an heirloom to cherish forever.
